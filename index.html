Dagorhir Empire of the Southern Star

Sandidar is the name our Dagorhir group uses, there are many other groups all over the country and even many scattered across the world. Dagorhir Battle Games combines fast-paced full-contact combat simulation with elements of live action role-playing. It also combines elements of both history and fantasy, because of this it is not uncommon to see a Roman Legionary do battle against an orc chieftain. Dagorhir is both a sport and a game allowing for people of many different backgrounds, ages, and hobbies to come together and have fun.
